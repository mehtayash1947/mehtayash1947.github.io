Education

  • Pre-College:

I attended 3 different schools before college, each starkly different in its culture, exposing me to various kinds of people, and I learnt a lot from all of that.

A 4-year journey at Bothra Classes, a coaching institute for IIT-JEE, groomed my academic mind and helped me crack IIT-JEE and get into IIT Madras.

  • IIT Madras:

My experience here has been bittersweet. Fantastic institution as it is, I made fabulous and strongly-etched memories within my short 7-month sojourn here. But the Covid pandemic spoilt the show and it still stings how we were deprived of 1.5+ (and counting) years of our college life.

But I do cherish the memories I made here, some of which I have mentioned in the Miscellaneous section. I had joined IIT Madras as a Mechanical Engineer but changed my branch to Electrical Engineering, which was great because I am much happier here.

If you only wish to look at my academic record rather than read all this, you can straightaway head over here.
