I don't remember most of my travels before I passed 12th standard. It's partly because I don't have many pictures of them, partly because I hadn't had very strong experiences then and wasn't too fond of travelling then to be honest, and partly because it's simply been a long time.

Travelling and intra-city roaming took a Quantum leap after giving JEE Advanced, which still continue to this day. I have spent a mammoth amount of time roaming in Ahmedabad and in IIT Madras.

I am extremely fond of Himalayan trekking. I have been on 2 treks till now : DC (Deoriatal-Chandrashila) and Rupin Pass. They were absolutely sensational experiences. They are the only ones worth mentioning here :)

Deoriatal-Chandrashila :

Although I have technically done trekking in Manali (in 8th grade) and Shimla (in 11th grade), that is not a genuine high-altitude trek. Those are hill stations and nowadays, no more than picnic spots.
