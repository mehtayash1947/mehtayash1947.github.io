It saddens me that words like "Spirituality", "Meditation", "Yoga", "Tantra", etc. have been highly contaminated today. I'd much rather prefer to call them "Inner Exploration". These things have been a part of India since very long, and over the course of time, a lot of misinterpretation due to lack of context and ignorance has happened in these domains.

So, to understand this domain of life in the correct context, it is necessary to tread cautiously and not to have any pre-conceived stereotypes.

Befitting this, the first blog I have written is precisely on this topic. It is quite an eye-opener on how to approach this domain of life.
